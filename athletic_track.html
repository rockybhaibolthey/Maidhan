How We Build

1. Sub-base & Drainage
Compacted granular base with precise grading and subsurface drains to ensure structural stability and longevity.
2. Surface Layering
Spray-applied polyurethane (Tartan), EPDM, or poured-in-place rubber systems for consistent grip and shock absorption.
3. Markings & Equipment
Durable, precise lane and field markings, starting blocks, hurdles anchors and field event zones installed to specification.

Surface Types

Polyurethane (Tartan / Poured)
Competition-grade, spray or poured systems with excellent durability, grip and uniformity.
EPDM Granule
Coloured rubber granules bound with polyurethane – cost-effective, cushioned option for schools and clubs.
Cinder or Stone Chip
Traditional surface for training; lower cost but higher maintenance.
Modular Synthetic
Interlocking tiles for quick installation and multi-use facilities.
