How We Build

1. Court Layout & Clearances
Standard play area (18m x 9m) with recommended free zone and spectator sightlines. We design safe run-off and surface transitions.
2. Surfaces & Net Systems
Indoor sport flooring (cushioned multi-layer), outdoor acrylic or sand depth for beach courts, and secure posts with padding.
3. Lighting & Equipment
Glare-controlled LED lighting, adjustable net tension, antennas and referee stand placement for competitive play.
