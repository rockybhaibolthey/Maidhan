How We Build

1. Court Layout & Markings
We build pickleball courts to official size, with clearly marked lines and the correct non-volley zones. Extra space is planned around the court so players can move safely and comfortably.
2. Court Surface & Net
We use smooth, durable sport surfaces that give good grip and consistent ball bounce. Regulation-height nets are installed securely for reliable play at every level.
3. Lighting & Court Features
We add bright, glare-controlled lighting so games can continue into the evening. Finishing touches like fencing, seating, and court accessories help create a complete and welcoming space.
