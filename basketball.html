How We Build Your Basketball Court

1. Sub-base & Drainage
We prepare a strong, compacted base with smart drainage to prevent cracks, puddles, and uneven surfaces.
2. Surface & Lines
Pick the right surface: indoor synthetic, modular tiles, or outdoor asphalt — all with accurate lines for training and games.
3. Equipment & Safety
We install secure hoops, padding, proper clearance, and lighting to ensure safety and a great playing experience.
