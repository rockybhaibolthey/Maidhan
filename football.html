How We Build

Every football ground we build follows a structured and reliable process to ensure longevity, quality, and excellent play experience:

  • Site Analysis & Planning: We assess the terrain, drainage, and space availability to plan your turf precisely.
  • Ground Preparation: Leveling, compaction, and base layer construction for solid foundation.
  • Drainage System Installation: Proper water management to prevent flooding or waterlogging.
  • Artificial Grass Laying: High-quality, durable turf installation with line markings.
  • Perimeter Fencing & Lighting: Optional additions to secure and illuminate the space.
  • Final Inspection & Handover: Delivered ready-to-play, with full quality assurance.
