How We Build Your Hockey Surface

1. Site Inspection & Planning
We visit your location to understand ground conditions, space availability, usage needs, and budget. Based on this, we recommend the right surface (turf, artificial grass, indoor smooth, or hard court).
2. Base Work & Drainage
A strong foundation is created using proper leveling, stone layers, and drainage systems. This prevents water stagnation, uneven surfaces, and increases the life of the hockey court.
3. Surface Installation & Marking
We install the selected hockey surface with precise alignment and secure fixing. Line markings are added as per hockey standards, followed by final quality checks to ensure smooth play and player safety.
