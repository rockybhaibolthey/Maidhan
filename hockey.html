How We Build

1. Site Survey & Prep
Geotechnical checks, grading, and sub-base design ensure pitch stability and even surface response.
2. Drainage & Base
Engineered drainage and fine-graded stone base reduce water pooling and preserve surface integrity.
3. Surface Installation
Water-based, sand-dressed or sand-filled carpets installed and line-marked to specification with secure shock-pad options where required.

Surface Types

Water-based
Premium competition surface — needs irrigation before play but delivers true ball roll and player speed.
Sand-dressed
Good balance of speed and cushioning — popular for multi-use venues.
Sand-filled
Cost-effective with good wear resistance for club-level play.
Natural Grass / Hybrid
Restoration or hybrid turf systems for venues preferring natural aesthetics and playability.
